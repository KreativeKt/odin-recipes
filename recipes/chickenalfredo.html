Garlic Knot Chicken Alfredo

Description

Originally as you might see from the picture this recipe was made to be a ring. I chose not to do it that way because there isn't a bundt pan in my kitchen currently. The first time making this, I followed it strictly how tasty showed me but just added my own seasonings and personally found that it could be better. This recipe also uses store-bought alfredo but feel free to make your own!

Ingredients

  • Two packs pizza dough
  • Two cups chicken, cooked and shredded
  • One cup bacon, cooked and chopped
  • Two cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • One cup grated parm cheese
  • One whole jar of alfredo sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375F
  2. Cook bacon, cut up and set aside
  3. Cook chicken and season, shred and set aside
  4. In a large bowl, combine chicken, bacon, mozzarella, parmesan, seasonings and at least one cup alfredo sauce; MIX WELL
  5. Scoop mixture evenly into a greased 9-inch cake pan
  6. Roll pizza dough into balls, with butter and seasonings
  7. Drop balls of pizza dough on top of mixture in pan
  8. Bake for 45-55 minutes, until top is golden
