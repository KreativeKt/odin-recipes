Spaghetti

Description

Most people tend to use hamburger meat in their spaghetti but my preference is turkey. On average I tend not to use any meatballs, for no real reason as they are enjoyed by my household. I haven't tried making my own tomato sauce yet but really would like too.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple sausage from Lowe's Foods
  • One lb turkey
  • Two packs noodles
  • One diced onion
  • A lot of different seasonings
  • Butter
  • Cooking oil
  • Two pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil
  2. Add in pinch of salt, noodles to boiling water
  3. Add sauce to seperate pan, season and keep on low
  4. Melt butter in small pan, add onions and golden them, set aside
  5. Cut sausage into bite-size pieces
  6. In a seperate pan, cook and season sausage, set aside
  7. Cook and season ground turkey
  8. Drain noodles, drizzle with olive oil
  9. Add half noodles, sauce, meats and onions to pot and mix
  10. Add other half, mix and serve
