Cheesecake

Description

This creamy dessert has always been a huge hit in my family so it was only right that I found the perfect recipe for me and mastered it. This will work well with any topping of your choice regardless if you make your own, or just use a store bought canned fruit. It will a produce a cheesecake that you may think looks small, but the slices are thick and delicious!

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 7 tablespoons melted butter

Cheesecake

  • 32 oz cream cheese softened to room
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs room temp, lightly beaten

Steps

Crust prep

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Prepare crust first by mixing all listed ingredients
  3. Add melted butter to crust mix and use fork to combine well
  4. Pour crumbs into 9" Springform pan and press firmly into bottom & sides

Cheesecake

  1. In large bowl add cream cheese and stir until smooth & creamy don't overbeat
  2. Add sugar and stir again until creamy
  3. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt and stir. Pause to scrape sides
  4. Mixer on low, gradually add lightly beaten eggs, one at a time until each is incorporated
  5. Use spatula to make sure all mix is well incorporated
  6. Pour mix into prepared pan with crust, place on cookie sheet to insure against leaks
  7. Transer to center rack and bake on 325F for about 75 minutes
  8. Remove and allow cool for 10 minutes. Once 10 minutes have passed, use knife to gently loosen crust from pan
  9. Allow to cool for another 1-2 hours until near room temperature before transferring to refrigerator to cool overnight
